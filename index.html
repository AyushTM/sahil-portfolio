About Me

The Editor Behind the Lens

I'm Sahill Mishra — a passionate video editor who recently completed my 12th grade and dove headfirst into the creator economy. While most people my age were figuring things out, I was cutting footage and building something real.

My obsession is storytelling. I believe every piece of raw footage has a story waiting to be told, and my job is to find it — through tight editing, perfect pacing, sound design, and visual rhythm that keeps audiences watching until the very end.

I specialize in short-form content — the kind that stops the scroll. Reels, YouTube Shorts, talking-head videos, and cinematic edits that feel premium. I'm doing it all transparently, documenting every win and loss of my ₹1,00,000 challenge publicly.
